Canned Food Drive

Throughout the month of November, STEP For A Better World LEO Club led a canned food drive for the Homefront. We would like to thank the following people for donating: Website Designer Nimeesh Sharma (50 cans), Volunteer Parth Sharma (50 cans), Leo Krish Usoghankar (24 cans), Leo Savvy Sridhar (24 cans), Leo Aarnav Hariramani (24 cans), Leo Anikait Sengupta (20 cans), Volunteer Ishir Jhunjhunwala (18 cans), Mentor Akshata Padalkar (16 cans), Advisor Gloria Shah(10 cans), Leo Srinidhi Sivakumar (14 cans), President Surya Sivakumar (14 cans), Leo Sanvi Jain (12 cans), Leo Ronak Shah (10 cans), Mentor Rahul Shah (9 cans), Leo Sushant Chanda (8 cans), Leo Deepanshu Kaushik (8 cans), Leo Akshara Thogarchedu (8 cans), Photographer Zachary Schwartz (7 cans), Leo Nikhil Kanuganti (6 cans), Leo Shinaya Jain (6 cans), Advisor Jessica Shah (5 cans), Health and Enviromental Chairman Aditya Davanam (5 cans), Vice President Pranav Joshi (5 cans), Volunteer Ishaan Joshi (5 cans), and Video Editor Neil Bhuteja (4 cans). Thank you to members of the community Rahul Shah (9 cans), Mrs. Singh (6 cans), Thomas (5 cans), Rea (6 cans), and the anonymous donors who donated 5 cans. On November 20th, STEP For A Better World LEO Club used monetary donations to buy 19 more cans. Thank you to Secretary Diya Patel and Leo Riddhima Koka for their monetary donations. We would also like to thank Advisor Jessica Shah, President Surya Sivakumar, and Website Developer Nimeesh Sharma for going to purchase the cans. Also a huge thank you is in order to everybody for their hard work in making sure that resources go out to people in need. Your work is appreciated! Lastly thank you to Website Developer Nimeesh Sharma for leading this drive!
