LDUN Event

On March 11th, STEP members attended the LDUN Reception at @westingrandcentral after the conference. LEO Advisor Lion Jessica Shah, Lion Gloria Shah, Leo-Lion Abhinav Pandey, President LEO Surya Sivakumar, Vice President LEO Pranav Joshi, Website Developer LEO Nimeesh Sharma, Chief Editor LEO Anika Bawa, Publisher LEO Ariya Bawa, LEO Veer Desai, LEO Riddhima Koka, LEO Tejas Jadar, and LEO Ishani Konar interacted with LCI executives and conversed with Lions from across the world, while entertaining each other with stories of their volunteering work. It was a great place to connect with Lions and share our mission, and our achievements. We would like to thank Lion Mahesh Chitnis for introducing us to so many new people. @lionsclubs Note there are 31 images, so it would be worthwhile to go through them all!
