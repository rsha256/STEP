Krispy Kreme Fundraiser

From July 8th to July 12th, STEP For A Better World Leo Club will be running a fundraiser selling Krispy Kreme doughnuts. These doughnuts will be sold by the dozen, and be available in two different types: glazed and assorted. Glazed dozens will be a 12 pack of plain glazed doughnuts, whereas assorted will be a 12 pack filled with glazed, chocolate iced, lemon filled, cream filled, and raspberry filled. A glazed dozen will sell for $12, and an assorted dozen will sell for $14 The expected delivery date will be on August 8th and a address will be shared for pickup. If you are referred by a STEP Member please pick their name and they will disperse the donuts to you. All proceeds from this fundraiser will head towards our Back to School Drive, where we will purchase new supplies to combine with our donations from the community to donate to the elementary students in the Trenton District.
