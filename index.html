        Mission          

STEP For A Better World Leo Club is a student led non-profit organization affiliated with Lions Club International and sponsored by West Windsor Lions Club. We started with a mission of uniting our community to help bring social changes to the world. We partner with various organizations located globally for many different causes, focusing primarily on aid throughout the state of New Jersey. On a community level, we provide a learning environment for young kids where they gain skills that help them throughout their life, and we help them use these skills to help out the community wherever they can. On a global level, we hope to change our society positively and continue to take steps towards a better world.


Vision

STEP supports the betterment of our community and the world especially helping the underprivileged. The club is not dedicated to any one cause but, is adaptable to all the needs that our community has. We will keep you posted as we continue to ​make our mission a reality. STEP For A Better World became a Leo Club and is now affiliated with Lion's Club International (A Global Humanitarian Organization) since we were Inaugarated on September 29th, 2019!

Safety

We ensure that all safety precautions are met and promote a safe, fun, inclusive environment.

Training

We train both members and volunteers to be the leaders of tommorrow by building their communication skills and leadership abilities. In addition, to fostering growth, being more proactive and confident in our communities.

Education

We educate and encourage our volunteers, members, and those in our communities too.

Partnership

We partner with various organizations to establish stronger relationships to help serve the community and advance our vision statement.
